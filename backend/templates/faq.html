Frequently Asked Questions

What is DnDez?

A Companion Web App to use alongside a game of Dungeons and Dragons.

What does DnDez do?

Provides a set of modules, such as Dice Roller or Combat Tracker, to help D&D sessions run efficiently.

How do I start?

Create an account is Step Number 1. Once that's done, set up a character in our Character Suite. Then, set up a room as Dungeon Master or join one as a Player!

What if I don't know how to play?

Check out our How-To-Play page, or, have an experienced player take you under their wing!

Anything else?

Immerse yourself, drink responsibly, and most importantly, have fun!
